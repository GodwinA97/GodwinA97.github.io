City of Leeds

Top Attractions of Leeds

Royal Armouries Museum

Founded in 1983, Royal Armouries is UK's national collection of arms and armour and oldest museum in the country

Roundhay Park

This is one of the biggest city parks in Europe, covering over 700 acres of land with lakes, woodlands, gardens, cafes and playgrounds!

Leeds Playhouse

This has been one of UK's leading award-winning theatres for over 50 years, and a cultural hub

Trinity Leeds

Leeds' premier shopping and leisure centre in the heart of the city, with ober 120 shops, cafes, bars, restaurants and everyman cinema

Leeds City Cathedral

Dedicated to Saint Anne, the mother of Our Lady, the Cathedral is situated in the heart of Leeds city centre. Place of silence, reflection and prayer

Tropical World

One of UK's largest indoor tropical rainforest attractions - has the largest collection of tropical plants and animals in Yorkshire.

Leeds Corn Exchange

Shopping mall with 160 year history - first established as a hub for corn traders and designed as a marketplace for independent retailers and entrepreneurs

Quarry House

A 10-storey office building, opened in 1993. A home to Leeds Playhouse, Department for Work & Pensions building and social-leisure complex
